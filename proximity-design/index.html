What this is

Proximity creates visual landscape concepts from photos of your real property. We show possible finished outcomes before any work is done. This lets you choose with confidence instead of guessing.

What we change (and what we don’t)

  • We change: plants, beds, mulch/ground cover, lawn edges, soft landscape layout.
  • We don’t change: buildings, hardscape, walls, fences, grading, camera angle.

You’ll see the “after” on the same base photo — so the comparison is real.

Why this helps

Most landscape regret comes from uncertainty, not bad work. When people can’t clearly picture the result, decisions get rushed and money gets spent hoping it works out. Seeing the finished idea first changes that.

Experience from both sides

Before Proximity, we ran a landscape design and installation business.

When one company both designs and installs, projects naturally grow and timelines tighten. As scope grows, it gets harder to notice where quality slips — and some issues don’t show up until later.

Proximity is separate by design. We don’t install anything. No upsell pressure. No reason to rush. Just clarity.
